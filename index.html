✅
The organic impact is minimal — and SL revenue is additive on top
On both platforms, organic metrics remain statistically flat after 7 additional weeks of data. App M1+VFM is now –0.12% (down from –0.76% at 8 days — converging to zero). Web is +0.22% with 22 days. Neither is significant (App p=0.57, Web p=0.39). The carousel introduces sponsored listings revenue that is entirely incremental — it doesn't exist today on MBNXT. The organic data now provides stronger confidence that there is no meaningful harm.

Recommendations
📱 App (MBNXT · iOS)
✅ Lean to ship
  • Organic impact is flat — M1+VFM –0.12% after 15 days (converged from –0.76% at 8 days), p=0.57
  • CVR now positive (+0.40%) — treatment users are converting at a slightly higher rate
  • Cancellations down –6.07%, refunds down –0.46% — SL deal quality is solid
  • SL revenue is fully incremental: any amount recovered improves net position
  • Connect SL revenue data from treatment arm to quantify the opportunity size
🌐 Web
✅ Lean to ship
  • Primary metric now +0.22% — organic signal has turned positive with 22 days of data
  • Desktop (web) platform: treatment +2.79% M1+VFM — strongest signal in the experiment
  • Web cancellation spike fully resolved: +0.75% (down from +2.76% at 8 days) — no longer a concern
  • Refunds down –1.02% — SL deal quality confirmed solid across both platforms
  • SL revenue is equally incremental on Web; connect legacy data to complete the business case
Next Steps
🔗
Connect legacy SL revenue data to complete the business case. The Experiments Dashboard captures organic M1+VFM only. Pull SL deal revenue from Jupiter/Janus for the treatment arm to confirm the ~$3M opportunity size — this is the one remaining piece before a fully confident ship decision.
✅
Web cancellation spike is resolved — no longer a blocker. At 8 days the Web cancellation rate showed +2.76%; with 22 days of data it has dropped to +0.75% and is within noise. No further investigation needed.
🎯
Run a carousel placement follow-up test post-ship. The minor Web CVR dip (–0.42%) is a placement signal, not a reason to hold. Testing position, size, and slot count can recover conversion — a clear iteration path after the feature is live.
⏹
Manually stop both GrowthBook experiments after the ship decision. Neither experiment auto-stops. Once a decision is made, the team must manually end them in GrowthBook to avoid continued splitting.
