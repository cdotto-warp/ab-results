What this test is
Recovery

✅
The organic impact is minimal — and SL revenue is additive on top
With 22 and 30 days of data respectively, organic metrics remain statistically flat on both platforms. App M1+VFM is –0.20% (down from –0.76% at 8 days — fully converged to noise). Web is +0.23% with 30 days. Neither is significant (App p=0.66, Web p=0.42). The carousel introduces sponsored listings revenue that is entirely incremental — it doesn't exist today on MBNXT. The extended run provides even stronger confidence that there is no meaningful organic harm.

Recommendations
📱 App (MBNXT · iOS)
✅ Ship
  • Organic impact is flat — M1+VFM –0.20% after 22 days (converged from –0.76% at 8 days), p=0.66
  • CVR still positive (+0.26%), GB positive (+0.31%) — no harm signal in any metric

  • SL revenue is fully incremental: any amount recovered improves net position

🌐 Web
✅ Ship
  • Primary metric +0.23% after 30 days — organic signal remains positive and stable
  • VFM +0.65% — strongest positive revenue signal in the experiment
  • Cancellations now –0.59% (fully normalized from +2.76% spike at 8 days)
  • Refunds down –2.09% — strongest quality signal across both platforms
  • SL revenue is equally incremental on Web; connect legacy data to complete the business case
